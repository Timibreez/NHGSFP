Our Story

NHGSFP

The National Home Grown School Feeding Programme is a Nigerian Government led, cost-effective school feeding programme that uses farm produce locally grown to feed pupils, with a view to strengthen, sustain and scale it up to 13 million beneficiaries by 2023, geared towards contributing to the vision of Mr. President to also lift people out of poverty by 2030.

Linkage to small holder farmers

Small-Holder-Farmers is of utmost importance to the continuity of the program. NHGSFP is linking small holder farmers in the value chain as part of efforts at improving the quality of meals for pupils, food crops provided at a competitive price to the vendors, as well as boosting agricultural activities and ensuring menus are designed based on locally available and produced food items.
